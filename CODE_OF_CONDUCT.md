# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Be respectful, constructive, and professional. We're building software, not arguing on the internet.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Trolling or deliberately derailing discussions
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to austin@iceglobe.io. Maintainers will review and take appropriate action, up to and including permanent ban from the project.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any public forum where you represent the project.
